About Me

Hi, my name is Moritz. Welcome to my website! I am a computer science student at the Technical University of Dresden in Germany. My research interests include microkernels, virtualization and formal verification of compilers and operating system principles.

I will obtain my diploma of computer science in 2024 from the Technical University of Dresden in Germany. Here, I am mainly affiliated with the Chair of Operating Systems and my work revolves around L4Re and the Fiasco.OC microkernel.

Previously, I worked as a research student with TUM-DSE, BI and Kernkonzept.
