## About Me[](#about)

Hi, my name is Moritz.
Welcome to my website!
I am a computer science student at Technical University of Dresden.
My research interests include operating system principles, microkernels, virtualization and low-level systems programming.
Currently, I work as a research student with the Chair of Decentralized Systems Engineering at Technical University of Munich.

## Projects[](#projects)

These are projects I work on in my spare time:
- Package maintainer for [`NixOS`](https://nixos.org/) – a purely functional Linux distribution
- Technical maintainer for [`Grünhelme e.V.`](https://gruenhelme.de/) – a "peace-corps" non-profit organization

## Education & Experience[](#cv)

I will obtain my diploma of computer science in 2023 from Technical University of Dresden.
Here, I mainly work with [`L4Re`](https://l4re.org/) and the [`Fiasco.OC`](https://l4re.org/fiasco/) microkernel at the Chair of Operating Systems.
